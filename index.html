Short Bio

I am a Senior Computational Scientist at the Brookhaven National Laboratory, Upton, NY. I obtained my PhD degree from University of South Carolina, Columbia, SC. Prior to that, I obtained my M.S. and B.S. degree from Chongqing University, China and Sichuan University, China, respectively.

Research Interests 
  • AI for Science
  • Adversarial attack/defence
  • Domain adaptation/generalization
  • Computer vision techniques applied in large scale scientific data analysis
My major funding resources are DOE ASCR, NNSA, SciDAC, BER, BES, and internal ones.
